## Simulation ≠ production (stated in the origin, restated here per the honest-limitations rule)

Every fitted config here is the **mock backend**, profiled as a "hardware" config for
**loop-mechanics purposes only** — never a claim about real GPU/CPU inference capacity. No
config in this report involves llama.cpp or vLLM. Any reader treating these numbers as
production capacity guidance is misreading them.
